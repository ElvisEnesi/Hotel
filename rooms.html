Our Room Amenities

At Tafari Hotel, we believe that true hospitality lies in the details. Your room is more than just a place to sleep; it is a sanctuary designed to recharge your spirit after a busy day of meetings or city exploration. From the moment you step inside, you will find a space tailored to your needs. Stay seamlessly connected with our high-speed, complimentary Wi-Fi, perfect for streaming your favorite shows or handling a last-minute work project with ease. For those quiet mornings, enjoy the convenience of our premium in-room coffee and tea stations, featuring curated local blends to help you start your day on a high note. Every element from the plush, high-thread-count linens on your bed to our curated selection of artisanal toiletries has been chosen to ensure your stay is as effortless as it is comfortable. Whether you are catching up on news on the Smart TV or unwinding in your dedicated relaxation nook, we have anticipated every requirement to ensure you feel right at home.
